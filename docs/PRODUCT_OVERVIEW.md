# Product Overview

WorldPeace DAO is an on-chain charity DApp and governance platform prototype.

The product combines wallet access, governance, treasury transparency, charity DEX concept, guild, chat, whitepaper, and Unity WebGL game entry.

The core product idea is to let a community coordinate around public-good proposals, small-donation concepts, transparent treasury records, and Web3 product interaction through a single DApp surface.

This project is an early product foundation that later evolved into C-Wallet, C-Chain, TRACE ProofFeed, and TRACE AI Platform.
